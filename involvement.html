INVOLVEMENT

The record label actively recruits employees and artists at the beginning and end of the small semesters. Students who are interested in being a part of the staff submit an application to the recrod label coordinator.

Students also complete an employment commitment upon joining the record label. This document outlines mutually agreed upon terms specific to the students position, the number of hours the student will commit, and a code of ethics. Terms related to the students compensation are also included if applicable.



As a part of the interview process, students complete a questionnaire assessing their skills. The students complete the same questionnaire at midterm and at the end of each semester to assess improvement.
